Our ATS-friendly resume template

MyResumeMakerAI focuses on one clean resume layout designed for readability, professional presentation, and better compatibility with modern online job applications.

Open the resume builder

Who this template is for

  • Students building a first professional resume
  • Graduates applying for internships and entry-level roles
  • Professionals who want a cleaner, more modern CV
  • Job seekers who need a readable ATS-friendly layout

What makes this template ATS-friendly

  • Clear section headings and standard resume structure
  • Simple formatting without visual clutter
  • Readable layout built for modern applications
  • Strong fit for AI-assisted editing and content rewriting

What to include in this layout

  • A short summary only if it adds real value
  • Work experience with action-focused bullet points
  • Education, certifications, and relevant coursework when needed
  • Skills that match the job you are applying for

Best way to use it

Start with your current CV or create a new resume from scratch. Then use MyResumeMakerAI to improve bullet points, sharpen wording, and organize your experience into one clean professional format.

The goal is not to make your resume look decorative. The goal is to make it easier to read, easier to edit, and easier to submit with confidence.
