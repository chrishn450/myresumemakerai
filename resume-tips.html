Resume tips for a cleaner, stronger application

A good resume is not just about design. It also needs clear wording, useful structure, and strong bullet points. These practical tips can help you improve your resume faster with MyResumeMakerAI.

1. Keep the layout simple

A clean resume is easier for recruiters to scan and easier for applicant tracking systems to parse. Avoid visual clutter and focus on readability.

2. Write bullet points with action and outcome

Weak bullet points often describe responsibilities only. Strong resume bullet points show what you did and what changed because of your work.

3. Use clear section headings

Standard headings like Experience, Education, Skills, and Projects help both recruiters and ATS systems understand your resume faster.

4. Focus on relevant information

Tailor your wording to the role you want. Keep the most relevant achievements, tools, and skills closer to the top of the page.

5. Rewrite unclear wording

If your resume sounds vague or repetitive, use AI support to make your language clearer, more direct, and more professional.
