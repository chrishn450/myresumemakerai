Frequently asked questions

What makes a resume ATS-friendly?

An ATS-friendly resume uses a clean structure, readable headings, standard sections, and simple formatting that applicant tracking systems can parse more reliably.

Do I need multiple templates to build a good resume?

No. Many job seekers are better served by one strong, clean template than by many decorative layouts. MyResumeMakerAI currently focuses on one practical format built for readability and online applications.

Can I improve an older resume with AI?

Yes. You can upload your old CV, review the content, and use AI help to improve wording, clarity, and structure before exporting a stronger draft.

Is MyResumeMakerAI only for experienced professionals?

No. The current template also works for students, graduates, and early career job seekers who need a clean professional format.
