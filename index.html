Should you add user reviews?

Yes, but only when they are real. Reviews help a lot with trust and conversion, especially for a product in the jobs space. Just do not add fake testimonials. That usually looks obvious and hurts credibility.

Add reviews after your first users

Once you have 10 to 20 real users, ask for one-sentence feedback and permission to display it on the landing page.

Best placement

Put real reviews between the feature section and the FAQ, or just above the final call to action.

Best format

Use a short quote, first name or initials, and role such as student, graduate, or marketing specialist.
