Resume templates

The best resume templates are not the flashiest ones. They are clear, professional, and easy for recruiters to scan quickly. That is also why clean templates tend to perform better with ATS systems.

What to look for

  • Simple headings and readable spacing
  • Strong hierarchy for work experience and skills
  • No distracting graphics or cluttered layouts

Why templates matter

A good template helps your experience look more credible. Even strong content can feel weaker when the layout is messy or hard to scan.

Improve content, not just design

Templates matter, but wording matters too. Use AI to sharpen summaries, rewrite bullet points, and fit your existing experience into a better format.
