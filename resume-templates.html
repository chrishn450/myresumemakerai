Why we use one template

Instead of offering a large gallery of designs, MyResumeMakerAI focuses on one ATS-friendly layout that keeps the writing process simple. This makes it easier to improve your content, keep formatting consistent, and build a professional resume faster.

Who this resume template is for

  • Students creating a first professional resume
  • Graduates applying for internships and entry-level roles
  • Professionals who want a cleaner, more modern CV
  • Job seekers who need a readable ATS-friendly layout

What makes this template ATS-friendly

  • Clear section headings and standard resume structure
  • Simple professional formatting without visual clutter
  • Readable layout built for modern job applications
  • Strong fit for AI-assisted editing and content rewriting

Best way to use it

Start with your current CV or create a new resume from scratch. Then use MyResumeMakerAI to improve bullet points, sharpen wording, and organize your experience into this one clean professional format.
