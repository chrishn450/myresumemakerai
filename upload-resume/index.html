Upload your old resume and improve it with AI

One of the fastest ways to build a better resume is to start with what you already have. Upload your current CV, keep the useful content, and let AI help improve wording, structure, and clarity.

Upload in the app

Why start with an old CV?

You already have useful experience written down. Instead of rebuilding everything from zero, use your current CV as a starting point and make it stronger.

What AI helps with

  • Rewriting weak bullet points
  • Improving structure and clarity
  • Making wording more professional
  • Turning older content into a cleaner final resume
