Resume examples

Resume examples help job seekers understand what employers expect. Strong resumes are usually clean, specific, and easy to scan. They focus on achievements, measurable impact, and relevant skills instead of long generic descriptions.

Student resume example

Highlight coursework, projects, internships, part-time work, volunteering, and transferable skills. Keep the format simple and specific.

Graduate resume example

Use a concise summary, emphasize tools and results, and show how academic work connects to real job responsibilities.

Professional resume example

Lead with recent experience, rewrite weak bullet points into outcomes, and remove clutter that distracts from your strongest achievements.

Use AI to improve your own example

You do not need to copy a template word for word. Start with your existing information and let AI improve wording, structure, and clarity while keeping your experience accurate.
