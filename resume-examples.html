Resume examples

These examples show the type of content you can write inside the ATS-friendly template used by MyResumeMakerAI. One template can still support different roles and experience levels.

Student resume example

Highlight coursework, projects, internships, part-time work, volunteering, and transferable skills. Keep the format simple and specific.

Graduate resume example

Use a concise summary, emphasize tools and results, and show how academic work connects to real job responsibilities.

Software resume example

Present projects, technologies, and outcomes clearly. Focus on impact, implementation, and readable structure.

Marketing resume example

Show campaign results, communication skills, and business impact with concise bullet points and useful metrics.
