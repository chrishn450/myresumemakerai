Resume examples

These examples show the type of content you can write inside the ATS-friendly template used by MyResumeMakerAI. Even though the product currently focuses on one template, it can still support different job types and experience levels.

Student resume example

Good for students with limited work experience. Focus on education, projects, part-time work, volunteering, and transferable skills.

  • Short professional summary
  • Education near the top
  • Projects, coursework, or student leadership
  • Skills section with relevant tools and strengths

Graduate resume example

Best for recent graduates applying for internships, trainee roles, and entry-level jobs.

  • Clear summary focused on goals and strengths
  • Education and internships highlighted
  • Project outcomes and measurable achievements
  • Clean wording with concise bullet points

Software resume example

Useful for developers and technical candidates who need a cleaner way to present projects, technologies, and work experience.

  • Strong technical skills section
  • Project bullets focused on impact and implementation
  • Readable structure without unnecessary graphics
  • ATS-friendly section titles and formatting

Marketing resume example

Good for candidates who need to show campaign results, communication skills, and business impact in a simple professional layout.

  • Action-focused bullet points
  • Metrics like growth, leads, conversions, or engagement
  • Clean section structure
  • Easy-to-scan professional presentation
